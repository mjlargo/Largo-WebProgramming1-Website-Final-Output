Frequently Asked Questions


How do I Inquire Services?

A: You can email us your rental request at dclorrentacar@yahoo.com or call 0920 957 3298, along with digital copies of our requirements. You will need to present them, so make sure to bring them physically when finalizing the terms of your rental.

What Do I Need to Bring to Rent A Car?

A: Please bring a valid identification card, your credit card, your translated driver’s license, and your passport.

Do Repeat Customers Get Special Deals?

A: We give repeat customers rental discounts, but those with good credit also get certain advantages.

How Do I Extend My Car Rental?

A: Please let us know how long the extension will be, and we’ll try to accommodate your request. If the same car is not available, we will try to replace it with the same model.

Are There Discounts?

A: We provide discounts for long-term hires, but for the rest, we make sure that our baseline rates are competitive.
